# Privacy Policy for Tasca

**Last updated:** December 25, 2025

Tasca (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a simple to-do list and task management mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use Tasca.

---

## Data We Collect

Tasca is designed to work without user accounts. We do not intentionally collect or request personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

The App may automatically process limited non-personal data, including:
- Device and operating system information
- General app usage activity (for example, task creation or completion actions)
- Crash reports and performance metrics

This data is processed in an anonymous or aggregated manner.

---

## Purpose of Data Use

Any data processed by Tasca is used only to:
- Provide and maintain core to-do list functionality
- Improve app reliability and performance
- Diagnose and fix technical issues
- Enhance overall user experience

We do not sell, share, or use user data for advertising or marketing.

---

## Third-Party Tools and Services

Tasca may use third-party services such as Google Play Services or diagnostic and analytics tools to support app stability. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

---

## Data Protection

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard data handled by the App. Despite these efforts, no electronic storage or transmission method is entirely secure.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Tasca does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If such data is identified, it will be removed as soon as reasonably possible.

---

## Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated revision date.

---

## Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the contact details listed on Tasca’s Google Play Store page.
